WHO WE ARE

Our Company

Go Trip is a leading travel company dedicated to creating memorable journeys and authentic experiences for travelers around the world.

Our dedicated team of travel experts is passionate about exploring the world and sharing that enthusiasm with you. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene landscapes of Switzerland, we offer a wide range of destinations and travel packages designed to meet the needs of every traveler.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, personalized service, and sustainability. By working closely with local guides and communities, we ensure that our tours provide authentic experiences while also supporting local economies and preserving the environment.

OUR JOURNEY

Company History

1996

The Beginning

Go Trip was founded with a simple vision: to help people explore the world and create lasting memories.

2005

Expanding Horizons

We expanded our offerings to include destinations across Europe and Asia, bringing more diverse travel experiences to our customers.

2010

Digital Transformation

Launched our online booking platform, making it easier for travelers to plan and customize their perfect trip.

2015

Sustainability Commitment

Implemented our sustainability initiative to minimize environmental impact and support local communities in all our destinations.

2024

Present Day

Today, Go Trip continues to innovate and expand, offering unique travel experiences while staying true to our core values of quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction.
