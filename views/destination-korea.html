Why Visit South Korea?

South Korea offers a fascinating journey through time, where ancient palaces stand in the shadow of futuristic skyscrapers and traditional markets thrive alongside high-tech shopping districts. From Seoul's dynamic cityscape to serene temple retreats nestled in misty mountains, Korea presents an enticing mix of fast-paced modernity and time-honored traditions. Add to this a globally celebrated cuisine, innovative beauty culture, and the unstoppable wave of K-pop and K-dramas, and you have a destination that appeals to travelers of all interests and ages.

Travel Tips for South Korea

üöá

Transportation

Purchase a T-money card for seamless access to public transportation across major cities. The Seoul subway system is extensive, efficient, and has English signage.

üëü

Shoe Etiquette

Always remove your shoes when entering homes, traditional restaurants with floor seating, and certain cultural sites. Bring slip-on shoes for convenience.

üì±

Stay Connected

Korea has excellent WiFi coverage. Rent a pocket WiFi or purchase a local SIM card at the airport for constant connectivity and translation apps.

üç¥

Dining Customs

Wait for elders to start eating, never leave chopsticks standing in rice, and be prepared for communal dining where dishes are shared among everyone at the table.
