Canada is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, diverse cultures, and friendly people. From the stunning Rocky Mountains to the picturesque coastlines, and from bustling cosmopolitan cities to serene natural parks, Canada offers something for every traveler.

Canada's climate varies dramatically by region and season. Pack layers and check the forecast for your specific destinations.

Visa Requirements

Most visitors need an Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) or visa. Check requirements well before your trip.

Stay Connected

Consider getting a local SIM card or travel plan for data access, especially in remote areas.

Tipping Culture

Tipping 15-20% is customary in restaurants, taxis, and for services in Canada.
