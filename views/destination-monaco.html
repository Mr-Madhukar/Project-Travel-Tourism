Travel Tips for Monaco

üí∞

Budget Accordingly

Monaco is one of the world's most expensive destinations. Plan your budget generously, especially for dining and accommodation.

üëî

Dress Code

Many venues, especially the Casino, have strict dress codes. Pack smart-casual and formal attire for evenings.

üö∂

Walking Friendly

Despite its hilly terrain, Monaco is small enough to explore on foot. Wear comfortable shoes but be prepared for stairs and slopes.

üìÖ

Seasonal Planning

If visiting during the Grand Prix (May), book accommodation at least a year in advance as prices soar and availability becomes scarce.
